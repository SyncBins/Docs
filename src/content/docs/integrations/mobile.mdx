Native mobile apps are on the roadmap. They will pair as devices, hold keys in the secure enclave where the OS allows, and support QR scanning for the six-word pair flow (the web app shows a QR placeholder but cannot scan yet).

## Status today

**Not available.** The [Mobile](https://github.com/SyncBins/Mobile) repository is empty. There are no TestFlight or Play Store builds.

## What works instead (now)

1. Open your SyncBins URL in **Safari** or **Chrome** on the phone.
2. Complete [onboarding](/get-started/first-setup/) via **Pair this device** and the six-word code from a laptop.
3. Add items from the mobile browser — same composer and drag limitations as desktop (no native share sheet yet).

Session + PIN behavior matches the web client: optional [quick-unlock PIN](/get-started/quick-unlock/), recovery phrase for full restore.

<Aside type="note" title="When apps ship">
This page will cover install, biometric unlock, background sync, and QR pair. We will not document features until they exist in the Mobile repo.
</Aside>
